This is Park Seo Joon, as captain Levi.

This was my first digital painting, I got inspired by the greatness of WLOP he is a chinese artist. I watched his youtube painting timelapse and I thought I can make my own digital painting using his tyle of painting. It was very hard because I don't know how to mix a color to make a new color, for example for the skintone. As you can see it was very warm on the darkside of the face of Seo Joon. The most challenging thing is making it more realistic, making a shadow while maintaining a realistic look.

The proportion of the painting is also hard, my reference is not well proportion to the dress I put, so I change the body proportion to make it more fancy looking and not look awkward.

Now the hair part, it was very easy compared to traditional art, in photoshop you can just make a hair in a minute while not scrubbing to much on the canvas using your paint brush, instead just use a fill tool in photoshop and make outline and fill the outline with the color you choose, just make sure the outline has no leak or it will fill the whole canvas.

At that time I thought that making a digital painting is easy, but it's not. You need to master photoshop tools and it's functionality, or else you're gonna be fuckd up.
